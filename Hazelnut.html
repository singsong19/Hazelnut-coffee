Hazelnut

  • 4 cups of brewed coffee. You can brew less or more and adjust the ingredients below accordingly. This recipe is for 4 cups of coffee.
  • ¼ cup hazelnut flavoring syrup
  • 1 tbsp. Nutella
  • 1/8 tsp. ground cinnamon (optional)
  • ¼ cup heavy whipping cream (must add)
  • Sugar as per taste. I’d recommend 2 or 3 tsp.

1. Once you have prepared freshly brewed hot coffee, take a large saucepan and pour the coffee in it.
2. Now, take the hazelnut flavoring syrup and add it to the saucepan. Also, add sugar and cinnamon.
3. Heat the saucepan and stir the ingredients thoroughly. Once mixed, pour the mixture equally into four mugs.
4. Take a small bowl and add cream and Nutella to it. Beat it until thickened.
5. Finally, add the cream + Nutella on top of the drink and serve immediately.
